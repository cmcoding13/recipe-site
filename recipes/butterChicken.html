Butter Chicken

Ingredients

Marinade

  • 800 grams boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 1/2 tbs grated/minced garlic
  • 1 tbs grated ginger
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp salt

Sauce

  • 2 tbs olive oil
  • 2 tbs butter
  • you can subsitite butter and oil for 4 tbs ghee
  • 1 large onion diced
  • 1 1/2 tbs grated/minced garlic
  • 1 tbs grated ginger
  • 1 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • 14oz (400g) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup heacy cream
  • 1 tbs sugar
  • 1/2 tsp kasoori methi or fenugreek leaves

Method

  1. Cut the chicken thighs into bite size pieces. Add all marinade ingredients and mix well. Let the chicken marinate for at least 2 hours but preferably over night.
  2. In a dutch over cook the chicken at medium high heat giving it a sligh char. Be careful not to overcrowd the pan and steam the chicken.
  3. Remove the chicken and set aside.
  4. Add the butter, oil, and diced onion to the pan. Sweat the onions for a few minutes. Add ginger and garlic and cook till fragrent.
  5. Add cumin, garam masla, and coriander. Cook for another minute
  6. Add tomatoes, red chili powder, and salt.
  7. Cook for a little longer and taste to see if there is enough salt.
  8. Here you can either blend the sauce or keep it chunky for a more homey feel.
  9. Add heavy cream, sugar, and kasoori methi or fenugreek leaves.
  10. Add the chicken and all juices back to the sauce and simmer for at least 10 minutes.
