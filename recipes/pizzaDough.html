Pizza Dough Recipe

This recipe will yeild two tweleve inch pizzas.

Ingredients

  • 153 grams of flour. OO or bread flour preferred.
  • 153 grams all purpose flour
  • 8 grams salt. (1 tsp)
  • 2 grams active dry yeast (3/4 tsp)
  • 4 grams olive oil (1 tsp)
  • 200 grams warm water

Method

  • Mix water and yeast together. Let yeast rise for 5-10 minutes.
  • While waiting, mix all dry ingredients together.
  • Mix all ingredients together in stand mixer or mix by hand. If mixed by hand knead for 5-10 minutes.
  • Cover and let rise for 3 to 4 at room temp.
  • OR
  • Let rise for 8 to 24 hours in fridge.
