Pizza Sauce

About 2-3 tweleve inch pizzas worth of sauce.

Ingredients

  • 28oz can whole peeled tomatoes. Many prefer San Marzanos but any will work.
  • 1 tbs olive oil
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2+ cloves of garlic microplaned
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • Pinch of red pepper flakes
  • salt TT (to taste).
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 2 basil sprigs
  • 1 tsp sugar

Method

  • Pulse tomatoes in food processor. Not pureed, still a little chunky.
  • Add butter and oil to pan. Once butter is melted, add garlic, oregano, and red pepper. Cook until fragrant.
  • Add processed tomatoes, sugar, basil, salt, and the onion cut in half or fourths depending on if it fits.
  • Simmer for 1 hour.
  • Taste and adjust.
